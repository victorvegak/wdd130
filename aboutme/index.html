About Me

Hello everyone !! My name is Victor Vega, and I am from Nicaragua. I'm married with no children yet. I am a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, and I have been able to have a closer relationship with our Heavenly Father. He has been helping me with all the challenges I have been facing in my life. The things that I love about the church are Jesus Christ, the Prophets, the scriptures, the activities, and the members.

I would say that my favorite job was when I worked with Microsoft; I supported Xbox consoles and games, and I had a lot of fun there. Playing video games is one of my hobbies now.

I'm pursuing a bachelor's degree in software development from BYU-Idaho. I want to support my family and become the first member of my family with a bachelor's degree.
